About Medical Data

There are three fundamental trends regarding healthcare data: it is growing, it is fragmented, and it is under attack. Meanwhile, forces like the increased consumerization of healthcare and the shift to value-based care and, of course, the current pandemic crisis have made the importance of leveraging the power of this data as vital as ever.

Users expect that any and all health data collected from them – or provided by them – become available to them through the various platforms they may use to manage their health

Platform task is to use existing technologies for analyzing big data and artificial intelligence to support human health and to give an opportunity deeply study the mechanisms of behavior of COVID-19 and similar viruses and to find effective methods of treatment and protection.

It empowers to rapidly create and scale the health care industry's next breakthrough applications.
